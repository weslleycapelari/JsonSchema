# Code of Conduct

This project is built on technical collaboration, respect, and clarity.

## Expected behavior

- Treat people with respect.
- Discuss code, behavior, and technical impact, not people.
- Accept review and disagreement with a focus on evidence.
- Use clear and professional language.

## Unacceptable behavior

- Harassment, discrimination, insults, or intimidation.
- Spam, provocation, or personal attacks.
- Improper disclosure of data, credentials, or sensitive content.

## Enforcement

- Maintainers may ask for tone adjustments, hide comments, or close interactions that interfere with productive work.
- Repeated issues may lead to restricted participation in the repository.

## Reporting

- If a conduct issue arises, report it to a project maintainer through a private channel or by issue, depending on what is appropriate for the situation.

## Practical principle

- Assume good faith, but keep clear boundaries.
